WHO ARE WE?

We are Special Boarding School students eager to help and empower others in technology. 
Our mission is to create technological solutions, enhance coding knowledge, and foster 
education in ICT.

Through workshops, hackathons, and hands-on projects, we provide a dynamic learning 
environment that equips students with digital skills. Whether you're a beginner or an 
experienced coder, ICT Club is the perfect place to grow.

Join us in making a difference in the digital world!
